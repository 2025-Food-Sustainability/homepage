Why Your Voice Matters

Every year roughly one‑third of the food we produce never gets eaten, yet it carries an outsized share of the world's water use, land use, and climate‑warming emissions. We're creating a roaming, zero‑waste pop‑up dining experience to help restaurants slash their footprint and prove that sustainability can taste amazing.

We're gathering diners from Australia, New Zealand, and the United States to tell us:

  • How you define "food waste" and what "zero‑waste" means to you.
  • Whether environmental impact influences your dining choices.
  • How likely you are to try—or pay a premium for—restaurants that waste nothing.
  • What excites you or worries you about the idea of turning food scraps into gourmet dishes.

Your answers will directly inform the menus, experiences, and community programs we roll out.

What's in It for You

Impact: Help restaurants slash their footprint and prove that sustainability can taste amazing.
Insight: See how your food habits compare with others in your region (we'll send a summary of key findings).
Influence: Shape the Slow Burn pop‑up so it meets real diners' expectations—maybe even yours.

We never share or sell individual responses. All results are anonymized and reported in aggregate only.
